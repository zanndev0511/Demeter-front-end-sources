All About Eatclean

Our Mission is Simple… Put Cheese Back on the Menu.

We believe in bringing you a completely natural cheese that is high in protein, low in fat and big on taste, helping you create delicious low calorie meals, or achieve your personalised diet plans. Eatlean Protein cheese is handmade using 100% British cows milk by expert cheesemakers on our dairy farm in Cheshire, and it’s bursting with benefits for your new leaner healthier lifestyle…

High in protein, low in fat, low in calories & 100% natural!


As the innovators in healthy cheese we are committed to helping you with your weight loss and diet plans, bringing you new and exciting products for you to try… keep watching this space.

Nutritional Information for diet plans and weight loss

If you are committed to being healthy or losing weight then the numbers speak for themselves with the Eatlean range of cheese Eatlean cheese is a high protein food which is also low in fat and calories. This means your diet plans are kept under control and you should also remember that cheese is one of the best sources of calcium. Compared to regular cheddar Eatlean cheese contains 45% more protein, 90% less fat and 55% fewer calories.
